Games & attractions

From quarter-eating classics to the latest racing rigs. Lineup rotates — here's what's usually on the floor.

  • 👾

    Classic cabinets

    Pac-Man, Galaga, Street Fighter II, Mortal Kombat, Donkey Kong.

  • 🏎️

    Racing & sim

    Daytona USA, Mario Kart Arcade GP, Initial D, motion-seat racers.

  • 🕺

    Rhythm & dance

    DDR, Pump It Up, and a Guitar Hero arcade rig for the brave.
